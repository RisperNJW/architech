Why Sustainable Architecture Matters: A Guide for Modern Designs

Sustainable architecture is a design practice that seeks to reduce the negative environmental impact of buildings by increasing efficiency and moderation in the use of materials, energy, and development space. Sustainable architecture uses a conscious approach to energy and ecological conservation in the design of the built environment.
